Assumptions

the premises everything rests on

AI adoption is mandatory, not optional. AI may not make a company more profitable, but any company that refuses to use it gets out-competed and dies — just as every business was eventually forced onto the personal computer. So adoption spreads to nearly everyone regardless of payoff, and token demand keeps climbing toward full penetration.

No breakthrough in video or physical AI. We assume self-driving, robotics, and other real-world AI see no major leap by 2030. AI stays focused on text, code, and background software agents — workloads far lighter on memory than streaming 24/7 video would be. The bottleneck stays raw compute (reading the prompt), not memory bandwidth.
